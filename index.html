How to Use

  • Hold respective emotion button. Samples are collected. Respected sample size for each emotion is displayed.
  • Try to bring variations by moving the subject.
  • A Sample size >100 is enough.
  • After collecting enough samples press the Train button and wait for predict button to come alive.
  • Press the Predict button. Predictions will be displayed at the bottom.
  • Caution : Don't overtake samples. Your GPU might run out of memory.
